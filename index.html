I am an inspirational and detail-oriented web developer with professional background in audio-engineering, video production and classical music performance.
To give a bit more background story - I come from classical music family, and since 7 years old I've been playing clarinet. After attending The Juilliard School and being part of Carnegie Hall's artist program "Connect", I decided to explore the world of audio-engineering, electronic music production, and DJ-ing. Having the pleasure of teaching Audio Engineering at Institute of Audio Research at lower manhattan and releasing my Electronic Music projects on various UK based labels, I decided to explore the world of coding and creating web applications, just like I did with music. Here I am today, tinkering things for best user experience on front end while making sure everything works properly on back end.

Aside from programming my hobbies include sriting music scores for contemporary dance production, filming, DJ-ing, and bench-watching old and new anime series.
