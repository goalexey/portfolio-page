PROJECTS

Happy Sampler

Happy Sampler is a portal for discovery of FREE audio sample packs and virtual instruments released officially online. Each page displays the list of specifically selected sample packs and virtual instruments picked by various blogs and yours truly.

The app also features a fully functioning MPC on the home page. Want to play some live drum and bass samples right in your browser? Go ahead, give it a try!!


Built with React.js, Node.js, Express and PostgreSQL.

Silent Hill Memory

This project is a memory game based on famous survival horror franchise "Silent Hill". In this game, you are presented with a grid of 16 cells. Behind each cell is a picture of a character revealed to you for 4 seconds and then hidden. You have to memorize the location of all the characters, and "save" them by selecting the appropriate cells.

Avoid the cells with monsters, or you will get a genuine jumpscare!


Built with HTML5, CSS3 and vanilla JavaScript.
Live
Github

Hidden Eateries

Hidden Eateries displays the database of great "hidden" restaurants and cafes around 5 boroughs of NYC. The "spots" were featured in various blogs such as Time Out and ThrillList and personally tried for their cuisine. They are definitely worth checking out. If you find yourself hungry, click on the neighborhood you are in at the moment, and see if one of the eateries is close by. You can also comment on each one to leave your review.


Built with React.js, Node.js, Express and PostgreSQL.
